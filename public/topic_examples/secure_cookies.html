Secure Cookies

you can set a secure cookie by appending ; Secure to the cookie string when you use document.cookie:


    document.cookie = "name=value; Secure";

The Secure flag only works over HTTPS

The SameSite attribute is also recommended to prevent the cookie from being sent in cross-site requests, which can help to protect against CSRF attacks.


    document.cookie = "name=value; SameSite=Strict";
